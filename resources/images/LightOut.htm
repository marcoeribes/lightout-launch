Meet The Team

Marco Villalobos Eribes

Co-founder

Dimetrius Hightower

Co-founder

LightOut began with a personal problem: Dimetrius and Marco were moving to new apartments around Tempe, Arizona for their university, yet they didn't know who their neighbors were.

We knocked on doors to speak to neighbors, but many would not answer the door, and those who did found it to be a bit awkward and weird that too young adults were meeting their neighbors.

This led us to build LightOut, which would allow otherwise turn unknown strangers into friendly neighbors that could led to life-long friendships.
